She went to Temple University, where she majored in Nursing. Brittany graduated with her Bachelors Degree in 2011. She spent nearly 5 years working in medical/surgical nursing. Recently, she started working in the operating room at Cooper University Hospital.

 

About JohnJohn grew up in Harleysville, PA. He is the youngest of 3, with 2 older sisters, Janelle and Kristine. He attended Oak Ridge Elementary School and Lansdale Catholic High School. He picked out a ring and packed it in his carry on for their trip to Las Vegas/California. John proposed at the top of the Eiffel Tower (in Vegas) on May 4, 2016!
